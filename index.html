About Us

At Fictiondex, our mission is to empower authors and elevate global literacy. As a mobile and web app marketplace, we enable writers and publishers to distribute their works and provide readers with a unique discovery experience through micro-transactions.

In addition to our digital offerings, we collaborate with local book printers to produce sustainable, high-quality books on demand, supporting local artisans in the process.

To further assist authors, we provide a suite of free resources, including self-editing tutorials, character and world-building guides, monetization strategies, marketing tips, and access to public domain fiction for inspiration.
